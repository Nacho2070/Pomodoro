What is a Pomodoro?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break work into intervals of time, called "pomodoros" (Italian for tomatoes). Each pomodoro typically lasts for 25 minutes, followed by a short break. After completing four pomodoros, a longer break is taken.
